Computers are awesome. And I love them! I was introduced to them at a very young age, so I've been around them for most of my life now. Gaming

This is something I have always been into. I have loved gaming since I got my first console (Sega Dreamcast). My first game for the Dreamcast was Zombie Revenge and I took every opportunity to show off how gory the game was to anyone who'd stick around to watch. Since then, I have gone through many consoles including the Sega Megadrive and quite a few PSPs. Currently, I own a PSP Go, PSP Phat, PS3, PC, Wii and a broken Xbox 360. I just love learning about gaming consoles and playing any games that I can get my hands on. Oh, did I mention that I'm a huge Minecraft fan? I can probably outlast in a gaming session. Who needs sleep anyway. :P
